1. Introduction

Welcome to Loop Chinese ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application ("App"). By using our App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, store, or process any personal data. Our App is designed with privacy in mind and operates entirely on your device without requiring:

  • User accounts or registration
  • Cloud storage or data synchronization
  • Analytics or tracking services
  • Third-party data sharing
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Contact information

3. Data Storage

All data, including your favorites and preferences, are stored locally on your device using Core Data. We do not have access to this information, and it never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to share it.

4. In-App Purchases

When you make in-app purchases (subscriptions), the transaction is processed entirely through Apple's App Store. We do not receive, store, or have access to your payment information. Apple handles all payment processing and may collect information in accordance with their Privacy Policy.

5. Third-Party Services

Our App uses Apple's StoreKit framework for in-app purchases. Any information collected by Apple during the purchase process is subject to Apple's Privacy Policy. We do not receive or process any of this information.

6. Children's Privacy

Our App does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no risk of collecting children's information.

7. GDPR Compliance

Because our app does not collect personal data, GDPR data subject rights do not apply. Since we do not process any personal information, there is no data to access, rectify, or delete under GDPR regulations.

8. CCPA Compliance

Since we do not collect or sell personal information, CCPA requirements do not apply. We do not sell, rent, or share any personal information because we do not collect any.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
